Background Of Our Operating System

Our operating system is the culmination of 6 weeks of work and >5,000 lines of code written by me: Stephen Simberg, and the BEASTS: Will, Jack, and Manas.
This project was created and tested in the last half of our Computer Systems Engineering course: ECE 391 at UIUC.

Website Design

The website design is based off the ECE 391 website and the open source code written by the wonderful Paizhang: MIT License.
Check out the Technical Overview to see actual work that was done.

How the OS Emulation Was Made:

This online emulation is powered by v86.
It uses the libv86.js, seabios.bin, vgabios.bin, and v86.wasm made by the emulator, alongside our own mp3.img hard drive disk image for our operating system.
